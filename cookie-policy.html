We use cookies to enhance your experience while visiting our website. This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and other tracking technologies on our website.

What are cookies?
Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember your preferences and other information to make your visit more efficient and personalized.

What cookies do we use?
We use Google Analytics to track user activity on our website. Google Analytics sets cookies to collect information such as your IP address, device type, browser type, and usage patterns. This information helps us understand how users interact with our website and how we can improve it. Google Analytics also provides us with demographic and interest data, which we use to tailor our content and marketing efforts.

How can you control cookies?
You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. However, please note that disabling cookies may limit your ability to use certain features of our website. We do not sell or share your information with third parties, and we do not use cookies for advertising purposes.

By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies as outlined in this policy.
